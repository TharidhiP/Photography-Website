All you need to know

Welcome! My name is Gamini Perera and it is safe to
say that I have come a long journey to stand here as
the person I am today. To give a little backstory about
myself; I studied in Ibbagamuwa Central College and
left school in 1982 to pursue my higher education in
Electronics. On finishing my higher education I was
employed at a leading colour printing and processing
lab in Sri Lanka as a Colour Lab Technician.

Fast forward to 1987, an year which I considered a
turning point in life, where I got the chance to work
in a prestigious company who were main suppliers
in professional photography equipment in UAE, Dubai
as a Colour Lab Technician. During my work period
of a decade I had the opportunity to work with accomplished photographers from countries such
as India, Germany, UK from whom I gained a lot of experience as well as valuable information, ranging
from handling branded cameras (5x4 large format
and 6x7 medium format cameras) and photography
lighting techniques. Within this time span I also got to travel and train myself in the art of photography in countries like France, Germany and Switzerland which helped my immensely.
In 1997, I decided to leave UAE and pursue my own colour photography and colour printing company with two trusted working partners in Sri Lanka where we specialized in colour photo enlargement, wedding and advertising photography. I am proud to say that I was lucky enough to work with leading advertising agencies in Sri Lanka such as:

Phoenix O & M (pvt) Ltd
Lintas
Leo Burnett
Saatchi & Saatchi
Bates ACIA or Bates Strategic Alliance
JWT
Grant and many more


One of the most crowning moments
in my life in the advertising industry,
2005 to be exact, was when I was
awarded five Gold SLIM Awards,
which was a moment in time that
I would never cease to forget.
The following year, Colour Beam Photography
Services closed down and I started an
advertising company titled as Grey Exposure,
which successfully runs up to this day. We
provide photography services in many
avenues such as Modeling, Industrial,
Architectural photography as well as product
shoots and we provide coverage for events.
Excellent services has been delivered to
clients locally and internationally. And some
of my clientele are:
And I have also dedicated a long period of
twelve years to photograph and provide
coverage for the Ceylinco Life Family
Safari to many exotic destinations such as
Singapore, Japan, China, Rome, Switzerland,
Germany and Australia which was a true
adventure if I have to word it simply.


With over thirty years of experience in the
field of photography, I can confidently
assure you that Grey Exposure can provide
you with the most esteemed services as we
are all familiar with taking an image, freezing
a moment and revealing how rich reality is.
